Peanut Butter Toast

Description

This is an upgraded take on the classic peanut butter toast. The addition of garlic powder adds flavor, sriracha adds heat, and nutritional yeast adds umami (and nutrition!). Perfect for a rainy Portland morning.

Ingredients

  • Two (2) pieces of your favorite bread (I prefer sourdough)
  • Peanut Butter

Steps
