Peanut Butter Toast

Description

This is an upgraded take on the classic peanut butter toast. The addition of garlic powder adds flavor, sriracha adds heat, and nutritional yeast adds umami (and nutrition!). Perfect for a rainy Portland morning. As my old roommate said, "I can't believe anyone who has seen you eat that still sleeps with you."

Ingredients

  • Two (2) pieces of your favorite bread (I prefer sourdough)
  • Peanut Butter
  • Garlic powder
  • Sriracha hot sauce (or any hot sauce of your liking)
  • Nutritional yeast

Steps

  1. Toast your bread (I prefer a light toasting, barely brown, crispy outside and soft inside)
  2. Spread a layer of peanut butter over the toast
  3. Sprinkle lightly with garlic powder
  4. Add a drizzle of sriracha
  5. Top with nutritional yeast
  6. Enjoy your spicy breakfast perfection!
